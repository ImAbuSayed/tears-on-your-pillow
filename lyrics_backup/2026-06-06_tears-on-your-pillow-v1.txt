[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I see the shadows dancing on your face
I want to be your favorite hiding place
Let me wipe away the memories that ache
And leave a tender promise in their wake

[Verse 1]
The midnight moon is spilling through the blinds
I trace the little lines your worries leave behind
Your heavy breaths are whispering a plea
To surrender all the weight and lean on me

[Pre-Chorus]
Just close your eyes and let the world go dark
I feel the rhythm beating in your heart
My hands are warm and steady in the night
To pull you close and hold you till it's right

[Chorus]
No more tears on your pillow, let them dry
I'm the shelter in your stormy midnight sky
Let my lips be the comfort that you need
And in my arms, your restless soul is freed

[Post-Chorus]
(Tears on your pillow) I'll kiss them away
(Tears on your pillow) Here I will stay

[Verse 2]
The scent of rain is lingering outside
But there is nothing left for you to hide
Your skin is soft beneath my fingertips
I taste the quiet longing on your lips

[Pre-Chorus 2]

[Bridge]
I hear the silent stories you do not say
Let me wash the bitter yesterdays away
It's just you and me beneath this fading moon
Saeed will sing your heart a sweeter tune
So melt into this moment we have found
And let the silent shadows fade around
